Draft an Architectural Decision Record (ADR) for the current context.

Include:
1. title
2. status
3. context and problem
4. decision drivers
5. considered options
6. chosen decision
7. justification
8. consequences
9. trade-offs
10. risks
11. assumptions
12. references

State assumptions clearly if details are missing.
